Hostel Management System


Kosi Hostel

Kosi hostel is build specially for M.tech and Phd students. Hostel provides excellent environment to research students. The purpose for providing academic ambience to the research students is justly met. Every student is provided with a study table, a chair and a bed. The room has an attached cupboard and book-shelf on one side of the room. Facilities for residents include gymnasium, outdoor stadium, first aid centre, Wi-Fi, photocopier shops, etc. Blocks are assigned with 24 x 7 Wardens/Supervisors, maintenance staff and Security guards.

The hostel is built in a manner so as to provide natural light and ventilation to all rooms. Filtered water is dispensed through coolers to meet the drinking water requirement of residents. The bathrooms have running water for 24 hours a day. The hostel mess functions throughout the year and provides healthy food to the residents. There is a reading room, a television room in the hostel for its residents.
