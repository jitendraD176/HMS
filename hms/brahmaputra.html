Hostel Management System


Brahmaputra Hostel

Brahmaputra hostel is located in the eastern part of National Institute of Technology Patna. The hostel was built in 2016. It is a 10 floor building which has 60 rooms at each floor. It is divided in 4 block (A,B,C,D) *with E block as extension. the hostel is surrounded by patna university campus from two side & third side is taken by local peoples. The fourth side oversees the Ganga river in north side. The rooms are allotted for two person subject to registration and clearance every year (for winter and monsoon semesters). Every student is provided with a study table, a chair and a bed. The room has an attached cupboard and book-shelf on one side of the room.

The hostel is built in a manner so as to provide natural light and ventilation to all rooms. Filtered water is dispensed through coolers to meet the drinking water requirement of residents. The bathrooms have running water for 24 hours a day. The hostel mess functions throughout the year and provides healthy food to the residents. There is a reading room, a gymnasium, a television room in the hostel for its residents. There are many sports facilities in form of a badminton court, a volley ball court, a table tennis table, a carom etc. The hostel has developed its own distinct charm and culture over these years. Students fondly remember their stay once they take wings and move out to pursue their careers. There are several groups and pages on social media which are proof to this.
