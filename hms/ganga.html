Ganga Hostel

Ganga hostel is located in the campus of National Institute of Technology Patna. The hostel was built in 2010. It is a 5 floor building which has 28 rooms at each floor. The back side oversees the Ganga river in north side. The rooms are allotted for three to four girls subject to registration and clearance every year (for winter and monsoon semesters). Every student is provided with a study table, a chair and a bed. The room has an attached cupboard and book-shelf on one side of the room.

Dinning


Multi-cuisines catering to vegetarian and non-vegetarian variety of Indian food items are made available to the students coming from diverse states and countries, within the hostel blocks. The spotlessly maintained vegetarian and non-vegetarian dining halls in the hostels serve wholesome, nutritious food to students.
A spacious Visitors' Lounge has recently been added for the benefit of visiting parents/guardians, supplementing the residential guest house facilities available.
